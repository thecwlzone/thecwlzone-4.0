## About My Blog

I infrequently write about technical issues, home brewing and the depredations of The State.

To set up a RSS feed, copy the URL by hovering over the RSS feed icon on the lower right hand side of this page.

For a complete list of my blog entries, click on the RSS feed icon.

Blog content varies. You could look in the [tags](/tags) page to view the various subjects on my radar.

No AI tools or Chatbots were used to create any blog content, unless otherwise noted.

I don't do Blog Comments. If you see anything interesting, please feel free to [contact](/contact) me.

## Privacy Policy

- I don't run any analytics, i.e. I don't track anything about you, except...
- Rotating server logs are maintained to protect the security of the site and to help improve the site by analyzing aggregate data. Your IP address is collected and retained for 30 days.
- I don't generate any cookies. Your browser may generate a storage or a permission cookie. I don't control that.
- I don't have any financial ties to anything that would matter to you.
